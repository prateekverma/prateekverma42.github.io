ABOUT ME

I am currently studying Interaction Design from National Institute of Design, Bangalore. My biggest passion is to design wonderful experiences. Whether that is through products, websites, apps or system designs.

I am also an urban planner and have worked extensively with governments on projects such as the Smart City Mission and Humayun's Tomb Renewal Programme. I understand the importance of intensive research and proper process for further implementation of a project to be accepted by people.

I love to eat, play guitar and talk about Design and Cities. I have a keen interest in Pottery. It has enabled me to understand how to translate real life characters into tangible forms.
